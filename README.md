### 💫 About Me

I'm a **B.Tech AIML** student at **LNCT Group of Colleges** (*2022 – 2026*) who spends most of my time on **backend and full‑stack engineering**. I care about **SOLID**, **low‑level design**, and **separation of concerns** so systems stay **readable, testable, and scalable**.

- **Backend & APIs:** **Spring Boot** — REST, authentication, **RBAC**, databases, service layers. **MERN** for end‑to‑end products and tight frontend ↔ API integration.
- **DSA:** **Java** on **LeetCode** — arrays, trees, graphs, recursion, greedy, sliding window, hash maps, and classic patterns; consistency over shortcuts.
- **AI / ML:** Hands‑on work like **customer churn prediction** and **crop disease detection** with **TensorFlow**, **Keras**, **Pandas**, and **scikit‑learn** when products need data‑driven or ML‑adjacent features.
- **Workflow:** **Git**, **GitHub Copilot**, and **Cursor** to ship faster **without** trading off structure or reviewability.

Previously interned and shipped production‑style features at **Xelron AI** and **We Win Limited** (backend, LLD, **Express.js**, **Python**, databases).

*Open to **Web Developer** and **Machine Learning Engineer** roles. Let’s connect if you care about system design, clean architecture, and real impact — not buzzwords.*
